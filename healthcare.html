Healthcare and Wellness

i) Infectious Disease Spread Simulation

  • Algorithm: Breadth-First Search (BFS)
  • Use Case: Model the transmission of diseases and determine high-risk areas.
  • Related SDGs:
    • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being – Focused on reducing the spread of infectious diseases and improving health outcomes.
    • SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities – Supports resilience in urban areas by identifying and mitigating risks.

ii) Emergency Response Optimization

  • Algorithm: Shortest Path Algorithms (e.g., Dijkstra's Algorithm)
  • Use Case: Enhance ambulance routing to ensure the quickest response times.
  • Related SDGs:
    • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being – Aims to provide timely and effective medical services to save lives.
    • SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure – Promotes efficient transport and infrastructure for emergency responses.

iii) Patient Prioritization

  • Algorithm: Merge Sort
  • Use Case: Sort patients by the severity of their condition to determine treatment priority.
  • Related SDGs:
    • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being – Ensures equitable and efficient healthcare delivery.
    • SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities – Prioritizes care for those most in need, ensuring fair access to healthcare.

iv) Patient Records Search

  • Algorithm: Brute Force String Search
  • Use Case: Quickly locate patient details or diagnostic information using specific keywords, including identifying health alerts or reports.
  • Related SDGs:
    • SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions – Promotes efficient health record management and access.
    • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being – Facilitates effective healthcare through organized and accessible records.
