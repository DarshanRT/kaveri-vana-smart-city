Healthcare and Wellness

i) Infectious Disease Spread Simulation

  • Algorithm: Breadth-First Search (BFS)
  • Use Case: Model the transmission of diseases and determine high-risk areas.

ii) Emergency Response Optimization

  • Algorithm: Shortest Path Algorithms (e.g., Dijkstra's Algorithm)
  • Use Case: Enhance ambulance routing to ensure the quickest response times.

iii) Patient Prioritization

  • Algorithm: Merge Sort
  • Use Case: Sort patients by the severity of their condition to determine treatment priority.

iv) Patient Records Search

  • Algorithm: Brute Force String Search
  • Use Case: Quickly locate patient details or diagnostic information using specific keywords, including identifying health alerts or reports.

Protection and Safety

i) Disaster Risk Assessment

  • Algorithm: Flood Fill Algorithm (BFS)
  • Use Case: Pinpoint areas at risk during disasters such as floods or earthquakes and plan efficient evacuation routes.

ii) Controlled Access Monitoring

  • Algorithm: Graph Traversal (BFS)
  • Use Case: Regulate and oversee entry into secured zones, such as government facilities or power plants.

iii) Emergency Call Prioritization

  • Algorithm: Heap Sort
  • Use Case: Arrange emergency calls in order of urgency, such as medical crises, fires, or security threats.

iv) Security Resource Distribution

  • Algorithm: Heap Sort
  • Use Case: Prioritize and allocate security measures for different areas based on risk, such as surveillance or patrol needs.
